About Me

I go by Krishna or Murthy and I am a doctoral student in Transportation Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin. My advisor is Dr. Kara Kockelman and my research interests are in the realms of travel demand modeling and forecasting through simulations. Currently, I use agent-based modeling to predict traffic and travel outcomes for Austin, TX. I got my master's at UT as well and my thesis was focused on dynamic ride-sharing.
