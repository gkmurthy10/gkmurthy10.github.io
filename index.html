About Me

I go by Krishna or Murthy and I am a doctoral student in Transportation Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin. My area of research is travel demand modeling & forecasting, and I am particularly interested in capturing the impact of shared and automated vehicles (S/AVs) using different simulation tools. I pursue my research in this area under the supervision of Dr. Kara Kockelman who is a well-known AV expert. I received my MS in Civil Engineering at UT as well and plan to strengthen it with an MS in Statistics in the coming years.
